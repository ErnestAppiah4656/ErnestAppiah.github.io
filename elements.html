Amazon seller order Analysis

This project focuses on analyzing Amazon seller order data to predict order outcomes and optimize operational strategies. The dataset consists of detailed records of online transactions, including product information, buyer details, shipping locations, payment methods, and order statuses. The analysis encompasses data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and predictive modeling to uncover key factors affecting order fulfillment and returns. The primary goal is to identify patterns and trends that influence order outcomes, such as successful delivery or return to the seller. Insights from the analysis aim to improve operational efficiency, enhance customer satisfaction, and reduce return rates. The findings support strategies for optimizing inventory management, refining customer communication, and addressing logistical challenges in specific regions or product categories.
